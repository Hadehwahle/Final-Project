How do I transition into tech?

Making a major career change, like a transition to a tech career, can seem overwhelming, but it is very possible if you break down the process into smaller, achievable steps.
1. Prepare for the change.
2. Research jobs in tech.
3. Choose a career in tech.
4. Learn the skills with Vephla institute.
5. Showcase your knowledge and build the skills.
6. Build your network.
7. Prepare for the job search.

How do I pick a course that suits me best?

Having an idea of what career in technology you're interested in will make it easier for you to find resources and guides which can assist you along your journey. Take some time to evaluate the different careers out there, and perhaps even try out a few online courses to determine which tech specialization interests you most. Is tech all about coding?

No it is not. While coding is a fundamental skill, the tech industry offers a wide range of exciting opportunities beyond writing code. So, whether you're interested in programming, design, data analysis, or any other tech related field, there's a place for you in this dynamic industry

What are the benefits of having a tech skill?

Having tech skills offers numerous advantages that can positively impact your personal and professional life. Let's explore some of these benefits:
1. Unlocking new opportunities.
2. Critical thinking and problem solving.
3. Increased productivity.
4. Enhanced creativity.
5. Staying connected.
6. Supporting social causes.
7. Making a positive impact.

How soon after my training can I get an entry level role?

Finding an entry-level job after your training is an exciting step toward launching your career! Ensure to prepare before graduation by attending job fairs, participating in network events and exploring internship options. These experiences can help you connect with industry professionals and gain insights into potential entry level roles.

Is tech only for the youths?

Tech is not exclusively for the youth. While young people have been at the forefront of adopting new technologies, the tech industry is diverse and inclusive. The tech industry offers opportunities for all age groups. Whether you're a recent graduate or a seasoned professional, there are roles for everyone.
